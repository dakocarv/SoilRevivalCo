Common Questions

What areas do you serve?

We serve Loveland and the greater Northern Colorado area. If you are nearby and not sure, just reach out and we will let you know.

What is included in the Winter Soil Revival Package?

You get a professional soil test using an 8 oz sample, a custom winter treatment plan, and a personalized garden plan based on the plants you want to grow. It is a $350 value for just $150.

How long does the soil analysis take?

After we collect your sample, results are typically ready in 5 to 7 days. We will schedule a separate visit to walk you through everything and apply your treatment.

Do you use chemical fertilizers?

Never. We use only naturally organic inputs. If it does not grow, we do not use it. Chemical fertilizers can harm long term soil life, and we are here to build soil that lasts.

My soil is mostly clay. Can you help?

Absolutely. We turn dirt, clay, and mud into living soil. Compacted clay is one of the most common issues we see in Northern Colorado, and our treatments are designed to address it.

When is the best time to start?

Right now. Winter treatment gives your soil time to absorb nutrients and rebuild before spring planting. The sooner you start, the better your garden will perform next season.
